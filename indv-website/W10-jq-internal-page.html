Kente is the most traditional cloth known to Ghana, and one of the most recognized African textiles. Men traditionally wear it during weddings and other festive or important occasions. Kente cloth is characterized by its alluring, bright, geometric patterns and comes in endless colors and combinations. Some variety of Kente exists outside Ghana, where modern artists put their own “spin” on the traditional colors and patterns to create an entirely different piece of art.
